Meet Fatzy

Nigerian born singer, rapper, songwriter and producer Frank Olusegun Okoro better known as Fatzy aka “THA REALEST” hails from delta state, born on the 19th September, he has slowly grown in the hearts of many with his music, he has released songs like “MAMA” , “VACATION” , “BODY” and also “SHE BAD” Featuring Erigga to his credits. Fatzy started his career as a group called (the zenith) but with a success, they all went solo after which he created and started his own group called (212 rap group) after a few years they all went solo and with no singles or album released. Fatzy is currently an affiliate and founder of his company called (212 Nation Entertainment) He has well over thousands of streams from across all digital streaming platforms and also videos on YouTube.
